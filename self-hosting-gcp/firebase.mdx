Disto uses Firebase for authentication. Follow the instructions below to create a Firebase project.

1. Go to the [Firebase console](https://console.firebase.google.com/). Click "Add Project"

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_home.png" />

2. Name your project "Disto". Click "Next".

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_name_project.png" />

3. Disable Google Analytics. Click "Create Project".

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_create_project.png" />

4. After you've created the project, go to the project overview page. Click "web".

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_add_web.png" />

5. Write "Disto" for your app nickname. Click "Register App".

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_register_app.png" />

6. Copy your Firebase config json as shown in the picture below, and save it to a file. Click "Continue to Console".

<img height="70" src="/images/firebase_config.png" />
