---
title: "Using Disto"
description: "How to see the fixes that Disto creates."
---

1. Go to the [Projects page](https://app.distoai.com/projects) and click on your project.

<img height="70" src="/images/projects_page.png" />

2. Click on the error. Disto shows the root cause, solution, and link to pull request with a fix.

<img height="70" src="/images/errors_page.png" />

3. To manually add an error to a project, click on your project, then click on Add Issue

<img height="70" src="/images/add_issue.png" />

4. Add the error message and the stack trace. Optionally, add the steps to reproduce the issue, and any additional information.
Click Submit, and wait for Disto to process the issue and add it to your project. 

<img height="70" src="/images/add_issue_filled.png" />
