## View automatically-created fixes

Disto automatically creates a pull request with a fix whenever an error is detected in your application by Sentry. Follow the steps below to view these fixes.

1. Go to the [Projects page](https://app.distoai.com/projects) and click on your project.

<img height="70" src="/images/projects_page.png" />

2. Click on the issue. Disto shows the root cause, solution, and link to pull request with a fix.

<img height="70" src="/images/issues_page.png" />

## Manually add an issue

1. Click on your project, then click on "Add Issue".

<img height="70" src="/images/add_issue.png" />

2. Add the error message and the stack trace. Optionally, add the steps to reproduce the issue, and any additional information.
   Click Submit, and wait for Disto to process the issue and add it to your project.

<img height="70" src="/images/add_issue_filled.png" />
