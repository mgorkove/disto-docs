## Share Projects

1. To share a project, click the + icon by the project, and type in the emails of people you want to share it with.  

<img height="70" src="/images/collaborate1.png" />

2. They will receive an email that looks like the following:

<img height="70" src="/images/collaborate2.png" />

After clicking the link, they will be added to the project and can view and add issues to that project.
